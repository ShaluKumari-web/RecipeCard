Choclate Cake

Preparation progress

Preparation time:45 minutes

Servings:4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions
